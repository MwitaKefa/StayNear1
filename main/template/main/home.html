About StayNear Homes

Kuhusu StayNear Homes

StayNear Homes is revolutionizing Kenya's rental market through AI-powered technology. Founded in 2023, our mission is to make finding and listing rental properties seamless, transparent, and efficient for both tenants and landlords.
